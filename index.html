Hi! I'm Natalie Parde.


I'm a recent Ph.D. graduate from the Human Intelligence and Language Technologies Laboratory in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of North Texas. My research interests are primarily in natural language processing, with emphases in human-robot systems, computational processing of creative language, and grounded language learning.

This fall, I will be joining the University of Illinois at Chicago as an Assistant Professor of Computer Science.
