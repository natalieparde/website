Hi! I'm Natalie Parde.


I'm a Ph.D. candidate and member of the Human Intelligence and Language Technologies Laboratory in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of North Texas. My research interests are primarily in natural language processing, cognitive science, and machine learning. My work is currently supported by a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship. In my spare time I serve as President of UNT Women in Computing, and (in my even sparer time) I also enjoy reading and writing creative fiction.
