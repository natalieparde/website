Hi! I'm Natalie Parde.


I'm an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago, and Co-Director of the UIC Natural Language Processing Laboratory. My research interests are primarily in natural language processing, with emphases in human-robot systems, computational processing of creative language, and grounded language learning.

To connect with me, find me on Twitter (@NatalieParde) or email me at: parde {at} uic {dot} edu. If you're a student interested in working with me, please read my note to prospective students.
